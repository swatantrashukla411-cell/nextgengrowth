Founder Intro

The person behind the platform.

NextGenGrowth is founded and currently operated by Swatantra Shukla, a student builder focused on giving skilled young creators real project opportunities while helping brands execute faster.

Not a big agency. Not a crowded team. A focused platform with direct ownership.
Vision

Make student talent the easiest growth engine for modern brands.

The vision is simple: Indian students should not wait for permission to build real portfolios, and brands should not struggle to find motivated creative talent. NextGenGrowth sits between both sides with structure, verification and clear project flow.

1

Opportunity for students

Students get access to practical work in editing, design, marketing, content, development and brand operations.

2

Premium output for brands

Brands get fresh thinking, fast delivery and project-ready talent without the heavy process of traditional agencies.

3

Individual accountability

At this stage, NextGenGrowth is run individually so decisions, communication and quality checks stay close and clear.

What NextGenGrowth Enables

A premium platform for small projects with serious business impact.

Inspired by modern startup landing pages, the experience is built around one clear action: help brands post work and help students find paid opportunities that build proof.

01

Creative Projects

Reels, YouTube edits, motion graphics, social media creatives, posters and campaign assets for brands that need attention quickly.

02

Digital Builds

Landing pages, web dashboards, basic automation, product pages and launch-ready digital experiences for practical growth needs.

03

Student Hiring

Brands can post projects, review student profiles and move from requirement to execution with a cleaner, mobile-first workflow.
