Privacy Policy

Your data should be handled with clarity and care.

This policy explains what NextGenGrowth collects, why we collect it, and how we protect information for students, brands, and platform administrators.

Last updated: May 13, 2026 Applies to nextgengrowth.in
On this page Overview Information we collect How we use information Sharing and disclosure Payments and KYC Security and retention Your choices Contact

1. Overview

NextGenGrowth is a platform that connects brands with skilled students for digital projects, learning opportunities, communication, submissions, and payments. We only collect information that helps us run the platform safely and professionally.

This policy applies when you visit our website, create an account, post or apply to projects, use dashboards, submit work, contact support, complete payout KYC, or use our AI assistant.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, role, password hash, Google sign-in ID where applicable, avatar, college, year, company name, and brand links.
  • Profile information: skills, bio, portfolio links, LinkedIn links, service needs, student work history, application history, ratings, and public profile details you choose to provide.
  • Project and workspace information: project descriptions, budgets, applications, acceptance decisions, resources, submission notes, submission links, and delivery status.
  • Payment and payout information: payment order details, payment status, Razorpay payment IDs, payout KYC status, bank account holder name, bank name, IFSC, masked bank account number, UPI ID, and encrypted bank account details where required.
  • Support and communication: messages you send to us, email delivery logs, OTP verification records, and AI assistant messages.
  • Technical data: basic device, browser, log, and security information that helps us operate and protect the service.

3. How we use information

  • To create accounts, verify email addresses, authenticate users, and keep dashboards working.
  • To match students with projects, show applications to brands, and show relevant project opportunities to students.
  • To manually verify brand accounts and review student payout KYC before payouts.
  • To create payment orders, verify payment signatures, maintain transaction records, and update student earnings.
  • To send important emails such as OTPs, application updates, workspace notifications, payment confirmations, and support replies.
  • To improve safety, prevent abuse, debug errors, and maintain platform reliability.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data in these situations:

  • With other platform users: brands can see student application and profile details needed to evaluate talent; students can see relevant brand and project details.
  • With service providers: payment processors, email providers, hosting providers, analytics/security tools, and AI infrastructure used to operate the platform.
  • For legal and safety reasons: when required by law, to enforce our terms, prevent fraud, protect users, or respond to valid government or legal requests.

5. Payments and payout KYC

Brand payments are processed through Razorpay or similar payment infrastructure. NextGenGrowth does not store full card numbers, CVV, or net banking credentials. Payment confirmation data such as order IDs, payment IDs, amount, and status may be stored for reconciliation and support.

Student payout KYC details are collected so students can be paid for approved work. Sensitive bank account information is encrypted in our database, while limited masked details may be displayed to the student for confirmation and to authorized admins for payout review.

6. Security and retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards such as password hashing, token-based authentication, encrypted sensitive payout fields, restricted admin access, and secure payment verification.

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal/accounting obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and maintain accurate project and payment records. OTP records and temporary verification records may be deleted sooner.

7. Your choices

You can update many profile details from your dashboard. You may contact us to request correction, account review, deletion, or help with payout details. Some records, especially payment, tax, fraud-prevention, and dispute records, may need to be retained where legally or operationally required.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or account support, email hello@nextgengrowth.in.
